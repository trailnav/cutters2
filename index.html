A range of Rings are available for the Disc Cutters ranging from high-alloy steel rings to rings with carbide buttons inserted and rings composed of a compound material covered on the tips. The ring's blade tip shape can also be designed and made according to the client's requirement or the formations. Any design of ring can be made.
LD Cutters - Supply cutter components for different disc cutters (ring) and components for cutter repairing. We also repair cutters or rings, exchange TIMKEN bearings, rings and sealings. For Rotary Cutters, Teeth Cutters, Breaking Cutters and Scraping Cutters - The carbide buttons and alloy with tungsten tips are imported or all the best of China-made. The cutter main body is made of high feature air hardening alloy, with heat treatment hardness HRC40~50. The main touching faces of the cutter body are covered with 3~5mm wearable material.   The carbide buttons, tips or blocks are connected tightly with the cutter body by the inserted method, or welded by the high frequency soldering and brazing with silver- base or copper-base alloy. The cutters and tips could be exchanged with the same made by Wirth, Herrenknecht, Mitsubishi, Komatsu, NFM or LOVAT on the TBMs. Any kind of rotary cutters, teeth cutters, breaking cutters and scraping cutters can be designed and made based on the requirement of clients or formations.
Rotary Cutter Series 6-inch rotary cutter series: carbide button cutters, welded tooth cutters and cast tooth cutters. 8-inch rotary cutter series: carbide button cutters, welded tooth cutters, cast tooth cutters; three kinds (shapes) of cantilever carbide cutters; three kinds (shapes) of cantilever welded-tooth cutters. 12-inch rotary cutter series: three kinds of carbide button cutters, welded-tooth cutters, cast-tooth cutters; two kinds (shapes) of cantilever carbide button cutters; two kinds (shapes) of cantilever welded tooth cutters. 6-inch, 8-inch and 12-inch series of carbide button cutters, welded-tooth cutters and cast-tooth cutters could be used to tackle different formations of 10-200MPa uniaxial compression strength with their long service life. 14-inch or larger diameter with multi ring rotary carbide button cutter series for reverse drilling: these cutters are designed and made to meet the reverse drilling and tackle the formations of 80-180MPa uniaxial compression length.
